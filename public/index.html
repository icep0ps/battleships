Battleships ⚓︎

  1. Place your ships on the player board. You can place them horizontally or vertically. Click on the ship and then on the board to place it.
  2. Once all ships are placed, click the "Start Game" button to begin.
  3. Take turns with the computer to attack each other's board. Click on a cell on the computer's board to attack.
  4. If you hit a ship, the cell will be marked with a red color. If you miss, it will be marked with a blue color.
  5. Try to sink all the opponent's ships before they sink yours.
  6. The first player to sink all the opponent's ships wins the game!
